'8 ounces/250 g stracchino cheese 2 (3-ounce/90 g) pork sausages  casings removed or bulk 8 slices of country bread  sliced '
'Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F In a mixing bowl  mix the stracchino cheese and sausages into a paste Generously spread the mixture on each slice of bread Place the bread on a parchment paper- lined baking sheet and bake until golden brown'
