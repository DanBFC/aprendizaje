'1 clove garlic  lightly crushed 5 tablespoons olive oil 2 tablespoons water 2 anchovies 1 handful fresh flat-leaf parsley  leaves picked and chopped fine 1 tablespoon capers Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper '
'In a skillet mix together the garlic  oil  and water and cook on a low flame Do not brown the garlic Add the anchovies and if you kept the garlic whole  you can now remove it Stir in the parsley  capers  and season with salt  and pepper Serve over grilled vegetables'
