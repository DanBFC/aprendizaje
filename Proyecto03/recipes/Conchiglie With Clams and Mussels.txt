'1 pound conchiglie (small shells) pasta 1 pound broccoli  cut into florets (about 4 cups) 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil 3 cloves garlic  minced 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 1 pound small littleneck clams  scrubbed 1 pound mussels  debearded 1 cup white wine 1/3 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley '
'Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat Add the pasta and cook for 6 minutes  stirring occasionally Add the broccoli into the pasta water and continue cooking until the pasta is tender but still firm to the bite  and the broccoli is blanched  about 2 to 3 minutes Drain pasta and broccoli  reserving 1 cup of the cooking liquid'
