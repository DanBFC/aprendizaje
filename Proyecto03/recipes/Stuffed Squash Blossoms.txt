'1 1/2 cups mixed fresh mushrooms  stemmed and chopped (recommended  cremini  oyster  button  beach) 1 shallot  chopped 2 tablespoons butter 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper Pinch Creole seasoning 1/4 cup fresh bread crumbs 2 tablespoons goat cheese 6 to 12 zucchini blossoms  depending on their size Basil pesto and Parmesan-Reggiano  for serving '
'Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat Add the butter  1 tablespoon olive oil and processed mushroom mixture Season with salt  pepper  and Creole seasoning Saute lightly for 2 minutes Add the bread crumbs and combine Remove the mixture to a mixing bowl Add the goat cheese and combine'
