'3 pounds/136 kg fresh sea snails  in shells 1/4 cup/58 ml extra-virgin olive oil 2 cloves garlic  crushed 1 fresh chile pepper  chopped 1 cup/235 ml white wine Salt  to taste Bunch fresh flat-leaf parsley  leaves picked and chopped '
'In a saucepan  heat up the olive oil and saute the garlic and chopped chile pepper Then add the snails Add the wine and cook for a few minutes Add salt  parsley and toss together well Serve immediately'
